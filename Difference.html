How is Treaty 1 different from other treaties?

  • The treaty was among the first of the treaties signed by the Indigenous people.
  • Treaty 1 had very specific geographic focuses on where it will be located, unlike the other treaties.
  • The prohibition of alcohol was rarely signed on treaties, but on Treaty 1, it did.
