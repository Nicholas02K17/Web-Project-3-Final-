Where and when was the treaty signed?

Treaty 1 was signed in 1871 on August 3rd and it was signed in Lower Fort Garry.

This treaty is important because it recognizes the rights and lands of Indigenous peoples in Canada.

Where exactly?

It was signed in southern Manitoba where Winnipeg is located.
